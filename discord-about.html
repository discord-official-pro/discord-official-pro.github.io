Our Mission

Discord’s mission is to create a secure, inclusive platform where friends, teams, and communities can communicate and connect. We believe in fostering a positive online experience where users can feel safe and respected.

The Discord Story

Discord was launched in 2015 as a voice and text chat platform for gamers. Since then, it has evolved into a versatile platform used by people around the world for various purposes – from gaming communities to study groups, creative collaborations, and professional teams.

Why Choose Discord?

With features like real-time voice and video chat, text messaging, custom emojis, and community moderation tools, Discord provides a unique, engaging platform that brings people together. Our commitment to security and community makes Discord the go-to choice for secure and organized communication.
